About

PayRent is a start-up company that prioritizes safe, secure, and convenient transactions while providing Filipinos with cheap, affordable, and high-quality services. PayRent is a platform for listing and renting local apartments that helps connect both landlord and tenant. PayRent also facilitates the process of renting out apartments with the use of microtransactions without owning any apartment properties. This serves as the primary product PayRent offers to its customers as a start-up company.
